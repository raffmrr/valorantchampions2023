Valorant Champions 2023

This is the third edition of the Valorant Champions, the crowning event of the Valorant Champions Tour (VCT) for the 2023 Valorant competitive season. The tournament is being held from August 6 to August 26, 2023, in Los Angeles, California in the United States.

  • Participants
  • Price Pool

Participants

Pursued by a lineup of 16 teams originating from four regions - EMEA, Americas, China, and Pacific - the event is organized into four groups, with each group composed of four teams.
